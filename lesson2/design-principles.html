Naver

In naver.com and blizzard.com webpages, I can found great proximity.
In Naver webpages items that are related are gathered together so
it makes easier to find things that I want to use.

Blizzard

In blizzard webpage, each contents are gathered together with related
contents such as games and news or new updates for a game. Through the
proximity they provide users where to find information what an user wants.

  2. LG

Google

In google, I think this page is the most well displayed the white space.

distraction. Since I love to use google especially while I am doing assignment.

LG

The webpage of LG is really simple and clean. It convay information simple by
