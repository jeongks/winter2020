The Salmon River trail is open for every one.

For your safety, We have Emergency rescue service.

☆It is not required to book service to use trail.

You will receive special compact device that you can put in your bag.

When the service has not been called you will get deposit back.

However, if the devide is damaged by you. it will be charged on your deposit.
