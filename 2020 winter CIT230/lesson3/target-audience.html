Target Audience

This website is for those who are going to have outdoor activity and who wants to know the severity of air pollution.

Personas 1: Jacob

Jacob is a 27 years old man who is graduated university and having degree in Computer Science. He loves to play sport outdoor. During weeks he works and on Saturdays he enjoys sports. Near his house there is a sports park. He usually go to the park to play sports. He spends about 2 hours in the park to play sports with his friends and other people. He only have that hours to play sports since he has two boys. This is the only hours that he can play sports. The two hours are only hours for himself during whole week. He really do not want to mass up the sports hours so that he always check the weather forecast for every Saturday. He need to find out accurate weather for Saturdays to know what he should expect for Saturday. He carries computers in his backpack while commute. He always take a small umbrella in his bag to not let the devices wet. He takes public transportation to commute.

Personas 2: Rani

Rani is a 33 years old woman who is having degree in Hospitality and Tourism Management. She is currently not working but taking care of her children. She usually stays at home. Once a day she likes to ventilate the air in her home. She tends to open up all windows in her room to let the air in and out from her house. She is always be with her second son who is 11 months old. she does really care of her baby's health. She avoid to open windows when there are so much fine dust in the air.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Jacob is going to find out weather on Saturday

Since Jacob is really into sports so that he check weather forecast everyday at kjgoodweather.com to see if he is able to play sports on Saturday. He likes to use the website since the site provides details of weather data such as cloud, wind, air mass, from the website. Those information is found by clicking the todays weather. When he checks the weather, he read more details about weather information by clicking today's weather. He can select to see weekly weather or hourly weather. As he click the weakly weather the calendar of the week be appeared with nice background image and brief information aoub the day's weather. He can browse to the map to see where clouds are located and where to move. Fortunately there is no sign of rain on Saturday. He checks the weather everyday with his laptop in case the weather changed. Luckily he will have sunny and cool weather on Saturday. On Saturday morning, he wakes up and open the curtain to see the sky. The sky looks dark and grey. He holds up his tablet pc to see if there will be rain today. According to the weather forcast it will be just cloudy in the morning and will be sunny in the afternoon.

Scenario 2: commute to work

Jacob's bag is full of electoric devices. He does not have a water proof backpack. While he departs from his apartment, he looks up kjgoodweather.com with his phone to see if he needs to take out the umbrella or not when he notice that the sky is not clear.

Scenarios 3: Rani is going to find out find dust dencity in the air before open up windows

One day after cooking, Rani felt that whole house smells like what she cooked. She decides to open windows to ventilate air. Before she opens the windows. she holds her phone to look up the weather information at kjgoodweather.com to check the severity of air pollution. In the web site, she can easily find the air pollution rate for the current moment. She wants to know when she can open the windows. She click the air polluation rate to see more detail inforamtion about the air pollution. There are different rates and categories of the air pollution. she finds out that after 4pm the fine dust density will reduce and categorized as normal. She decides to open the windows after 4pm. It is arround 4 pm now. She holds her phone and look up again the weather to check severity of the air pollution. As forecasted the air pollution is normalized. she opens up the windows to ventilate her house.

Scenarios 4: going out with baby

Rani wants to go out with her second son to refresh him. Before she prepares to go out she looks up the kjgoodweather.com to check the weather of the day. check the weather hourly to plan when she must return home. She looks up and gets all information that she needs. She and her son will have fun time out side.

Scenarios 5: Family picnic

Jacob and Rani plan to go on a family picnic on a zoo. Since their children love animals, they choose zoo as a picnic place. they are planning when to go. Jacob and Rani decide when to go. It will be on Saturday. Jacob will check weather and decide to cancell or keep the plan on Friday night. On Friday night, he can see the Saturday's hourly weather changes. thoughtout the Saturday there is no rain. Good to go on a family picnic.
